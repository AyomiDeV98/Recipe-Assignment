Classic Spaghetti Bolognese

A hearty Italian pasta dish made with a rich, slow-cooked meat sauce — perfect for family dinners or special occasions.

Recipe Information

Preparation time: 20 minutes

Cooking time: 1 hour 30 minutes

Servings: 4

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g minced beef
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 400g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat. Add onion and garlic, and sauté until softened.
  2. Add minced beef and cook until browned, breaking it apart with a spoon.
  3. Stir in tomato paste, chopped tomatoes, oregano, and basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Reduce heat to low, cover, and simmer for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  5. Meanwhile, cook spaghetti according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  6. Serve sauce over spaghetti and garnish with freshly grated Parmesan cheese.

Tips

For a richer flavor, let the sauce simmer for longer. You can also add a splash of red wine when cooking the meat for extra depth.
